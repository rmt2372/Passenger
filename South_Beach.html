South Beach, Florida

Rating: 4/5

Itinerary

Stay Here: Andilana Beach Resort

Immersed in a tropical garden covering an area of 15 hectares, with two magnificent beaches of white sand, almost 1km long, there stands the Hotel Andilana Beach, Madagascar’s most important tourist 4 stars resort. It overlooks the most beautiful and most exclusive bay in Nosy Be. The hotel offers its guests a stunning location for blissful holidays with their partners or families. The colors of the sea, the tranquillity of the beaches and the impressive sunsets make Andilana Bay the perfect location for your holidays.

Eat Here: Chez Nono - La Rhum Riz

This restaurant is a gem located a few minutes walk from Andilana beach. They serve delicious fresh seafood and include a reasonably priced par! The staff is extremely helpful and friendly who are happy to organize a taxi/tuk-tuk home.

Go Here: Humpback Whale watching/safari (July to November)

Yes, you read that right. A super cool activity: you can do a whale-watching safari here just like on Ile Sainte-Marie! Please note that you book this tour with an organization that handles humpback whales with respect.
