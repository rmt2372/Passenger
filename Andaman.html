Andaman Islands, India

Rating: 4/5

Itinerary

Stay Here: Barefoot at Havelock

This place is located on one of the world's few 'undiscovered' tropical islands. It has great walks and you can discover the surrounding beauty on a nature trek with your guide. There are also water activities such as world class diving.

Eat Here: Amaya Lounge Bar

Of all the restaurants in Andaman, Amaya is the most romantic place to visit, especially with your loved ones. It is situated in Marine hill, Port Blair. The rooftop ambiance overlooking the iconic north bay and the scrumptious seafood in the platter makes it a top-notch spot for having the best dinner of your life!

Go Here: Havelock beach

Havelock has beach No. 7 or Radhanagar beach which is voted the best beach in Asia and 8th best in the world. Considering its popularity, you may find it a tad crowded but nonetheless, it is a must visit.
