Anchorage, Alaska

Rating: 4/5

Itinerary

Stay Here: Alyeska Resort

Booking made easy! Click the link above to book your stay.
Alyeska Resort is home to activities and adventure year-round. In the winter, Alyeska Resort boasts deep snowfall and 1,400 skiiable acres for skiiers of all levels. In the summer, the Resort provides convenient access to world class hikes, fishing, glacier viewing, wildlife sighting, and more. Located in scenic Girdwood just outside of Anchorage, it provides a perfect balance between city access and unfiltered wilderness.

Eat Here: Moose's Tooth Pub and Pizzeria

Named after a mountain peak in the Alaska Range, Moose's Tooth is home to creative gourmet pizzas and local craft beers in a lively, casual environment. Popular with locals and tourists alike, it is also home to a vibrant live music scene with performances from local and national artists every month.

Go Here: The Chugach Mountains

One of the largest state parks in the country, the Chugach Mountains is home to accessible outdoor adventures for people of all skill levels and abilities. Climb to the summit of Flattop Mountain and get picturesque views, kayak on glacier-fed Eklutna Lake, or pick wild berries in Arctic Valley. The fun doesn't stop in the winter - trails are open for snowshoeing and cross-country skiing in the coldest months.
