Guacalito De La Isla, Nicaragua

Rating: 4.5/5

Itinerary

Stay Here: Antal Villas

The design of the Antal Legacy Villas was inspired by Nicaragua’s Spanish Colonial architectural traditions and Guacalito’s verdant coastal setting.The prime location of the Antal Legacy Villas offers guests not only a spectacular ocean view, but also close proximity to Tres Ceibas Members Beach Club and Guacalito Golf Course Pro Shop.

Eat Here: La Cocina Nikul

Dishes created by our culinary team grace our menus and bring innovative creations to our tables. La Cocina NiKul serves dishes made with local ingredients and many of the items on the menu reflect Mexican, Italian, French, Mediterranean and even Asian accents.

Go Here: Surfing at Manzanillo Point

Always wanted to learn how to surf but never really found the time? We are proud to offer services ranging from beginner lessons and coaching sessions to mastering the resort’s Manzanillo Point and guided half-day boat or 4WD along the coast. Guests can simply enjoy being taken to the best waves and secluded spots or learn and step up a level under a unique coaching plan.
