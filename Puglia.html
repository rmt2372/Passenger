Puglia, Italy

Rating: 4/5

Itinerary

Stay Here: Don Ferrante Hotel

Don Ferrante is a boutique hotel ideally located in Monopoli old town. Once an ancient fortress on the reef, now, an enchanting atmosphere welcomes the guests waking them up with the morning sea-breeze and looking after their comfort all day long.The stunning natural view of the Mediterranean Sea makes our Dimora and Suites, elegantly furnished and fully equipped, unique and exclusive.

Eat Here: Prendici Gusto

Go here for an authentic experience at a local restaurant. Dishes are hommade and presents a variety of food for meat-lovers and vegetarian-lovers. They do not have a set menu, every day are new dishes!

Go Here: Basilica San Nicola

If you love visiting historical site, this is the place to be! The Basilica San Nicola has existed for over 1000 years and have been the desitination for Roman Catholics and Orthodox Christians from Eastern Europe to visit. The story of its dedication to Saint Nicholas is an interesting story to hear!
