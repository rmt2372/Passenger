Montreal, Quebec, Canada

Rating: 4/5

Itinerary

Stay Here: Hôtel Le Crystal

Booking made easy! Click the link above to book your stay. Hôtel Le Crystal is stylish and modern, located conveniently near the heart of downtown Montréal for easy access to everything the city has to offer. It features friendly staff and luxury amenities, including an onsite restaurant offering authentic Thai cuisine, highlighting the city's multicultural heritage. It is also dedicated to environmental sustainability and is one Montréal's most eco-friendly hotels.

Eat Here: La Banquise

Poutine, the classic Canadian dish consisting of French fries and cheese curds, was invented right here in Quebec, and La Banquise claims to have revolutionized it. Offering over 30 different varieties of poutine as well as burgers, sandwiches, and breakfast, La Banquise features a warm, welcoming environment open 24 hours a day.

Go Here: Old Montréal

Old Montréal is the birthplace of the city, blending European charm with North American attitudes. Explore lively plazas and narrow cobbled streets, immerse yourself in old-world architecture and historic sites, and discover charming shops and cafes.
