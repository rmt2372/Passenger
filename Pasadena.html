Pasadena, California

Rating: 4/5

Itinerary

Stay Here: Hotel Constance Pasadena

Located on Pasadena's main street (right on the route of Pasadena's annual Rose Parade!), the Hotel Constance promises modern, sophisticated design in a Mediterranean-revival style. The convenient location, friendly staff, and colorful decor make for an exciting stay in beautiful Pasadena.

Eat Here: Pie 'n Burger

Exactly as the name says, Pie 'n Burger is home to iconic pies and classic cheesburgers with dedication to quality and tradition.

Go Here: The Huntington Library, Art Museum, and Botanical Gardens

In addition to must-see collections of famous works of art, rare books and manuscripts, the Huntington is surrounded by 130 acres of breathtaking botanical gardens to explore in 16 different themed gardens. Check out the California Garden, Jungle Garden, Japanese Garden, Shakespeare Garden, and more - it is home to over 16,000 different species of plants
